Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, navigate to the Profile section from the sidebar menu. Click on the Edit button next to the information you wish to update, make your changes, and click Save.

How can I track my delivery status?

You can track your delivery status by clicking on "List of Deliveries" in the sidebar menu. This will show all your current and past deliveries. Click on any delivery to see detailed tracking information.

How do I download my invoices?

To download your invoices, go to the "Customer Financial Sheet" section in the sidebar and select "Invoice". Find the invoice you want to download and click on the download icon.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.
